# Screenshot Guide for Connect IQ Store

## Required Screenshots (5 total)

Take photos of your Forerunner 235 watch screen showing:

### 1. Main Workout Screen - Active Jumping
**What to show:**
- Status: "JUMPING" (green)
- Jump Count: ~50-100 (something impressive but realistic)
- Timer: 01:30 or similar
- JPM: ~120-150
- HR: Your actual heart rate

**How to capture:**
1. Start a workout
2. Jump rope for 1-2 minutes
3. While actively jumping, take a clear photo of the watch face
4. Make sure all numbers are visible and in focus

### 2. Pause Menu
**What to show:**
- Three menu options: Resume, Save, Discard
- One option highlighted

**How to capture:**
1. While in a workout, press START to pause
2. The pause menu will appear
3. Take a photo showing all three options
4. You can use UP/DOWN to highlight different options if needed

### 3. Summary Screen - Page 1 (Total Jumps & Duration)
**What to show:**
- "Summary 1/4"
- Total Jumps: (your workout total)
- Duration: MM:SS

**How to capture:**
1. After saving a workout, the summary appears
2. This should be the first page shown
3. Take a photo

### 4. Summary Screen - Page 2 (JPM Stats)
**What to show:**
- "Summary 2/4"
- Avg JPM: (your average)
- Peak JPM: (your peak)

**How to capture:**
1. From Summary page 1, press DOWN to go to page 2
2. Take a photo

### 5. Summary Screen - Page 3 (Heart Rate Stats)
**What to show:**
- "Summary 3/4"
- Avg HR: (your average heart rate)
- Max HR: (your max heart rate)

**How to capture:**
1. From Summary page 2, press DOWN to go to page 3
2. Take a photo

## Photography Tips

1. **Lighting**: Use good lighting, avoid glare on the watch screen
2. **Focus**: Make sure all text is crisp and readable
3. **Framing**: Fill the frame with the watch face, minimal background
4. **Angle**: Shoot straight on, not at an angle
5. **Clean screen**: Wipe the watch screen before photos

## Optional: Idle Screen
You can also capture the idle/start screen showing "PRESS START" for additional context.

## File Naming
Save your photos as:
- `1_main_active.jpg`
- `2_pause_menu.jpg`
- `3_summary_jumps.jpg`
- `4_summary_jpm.jpg`
- `5_summary_hr.jpg`

## Image Requirements for Connect IQ Store
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Minimum size: 246x246 pixels (but higher resolution is better)
- Recommended: Take photos at highest quality your phone supports
- You may need to crop to square or resize after taking photos

## After Capturing
1. Transfer photos from your phone to computer
2. Crop to focus on the watch screen
3. Resize if needed (but keep high quality)
4. Save in `screenshots/` folder in the project
5. Upload to Connect IQ Store submission form
